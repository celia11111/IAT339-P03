Project Analysis

Overview

CallEasy is an App designed by me. The app is for providing a more convenient life experience for deaf-mute people.

Audiences

There are two groups of users for my App, deaf-mute users and signlanguage interpreters. By providing a online thrid-party platform, deaf-mute users and sign language interpreters could be set up by appointment and have a interpreting service online.

Goal

CallEasy aims on providing deaf-mute people with a better communication environment. CallEasy could provide interpreting services without the limitation of time, distances, language and price. Deaf-mute users would have more privacy than have an interpreter face to face. By using CallEasy, the descrimination would decrease since the companies and communities would feel easier to provide sign-language interpreting services and therefore, provide more opportunities for deaf-mute people to get into the society and live a better life.

Iteration

The color scheme for this app aims on having a delightful working and using experience. Since CallEasy is a functional application, I used blue and green to satisfy both of delightful and professional.

Design Challenges

Since CallEasy is a functional app, designers do need to think about the

function first rather that visual design. CallEasy is designed for deaf-mute people, therefore, I tried not to make the words offensive. Moreover, CallEasy requires payment so the privacy of users' information is also important. The main function of CallEasy is text and video call, which would work for both deaf-mute users and interpreters.
