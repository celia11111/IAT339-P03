Call Easy

A functional application that creates opportunities for both deaf-mute people and sign language interpreters.

An app enables sign language interpreter service without the limitation in location or time.

Explore more

Why Call-Easy?

Make A Reservation Easily

Simple interface to make a reservation and large numbers of available time

Anytime & Anywhere

A real-time sign language translator is an important mile- stone in facilitating communication between the deaf community and the general public

Inexpensive Fee

Cheaper than normal sign-language interpreter fee can help user save some money

Deaf-mute people

Sign language Interpreter

How Call-Easy?

1Log in

Easy log in and sign up interface and we make two different version for users.

2Make a Reservation

User have two option:Call now and make a reservation. Call now means user can find online interpreter immediately for urgency situation.

3Call the Interpreter&Communication

Interpreter translates sign-language to Deaf user. Morever, our app also has typing feature when having the video call

4Pay your order

After finishing the video call, user need to pay.

5Rate your call and the interpreter

Rating and comment can help interpreter gain home interpreter page first position
