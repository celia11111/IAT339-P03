Project Analysis

This is the 3D model I made for an idea of a flashlight. This flashlight is designed for kids, which tend to have some educational use. There are different sound effects and different colours of lights. In addition, there are numerous buttons that could be interacted, such as the eyebrows, the wings, the feet and the main button on the handle. I did some research for user experience for this project and I found it is good to use plastic or ruber for this flashlight.
