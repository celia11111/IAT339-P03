Project Analysis

CallEasy is an App designed by me. The app is for providing a more convenient life experience for deaf-mute people. There are two groups of users for my App, deaf-mute users and signlanguage interpreters. By providing a online thrid-party platform, deaf-mute users and sign language interpreters could be set up by appointment and have a interpreting service online.
