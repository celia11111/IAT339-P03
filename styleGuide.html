Typeface

Gudea is a readable, clear and functional typeface family, with a simple and condensed structure that brings a pleasant feeling when used at any size. Inspired by engineering documentation, it expresses the technical feeling of graphic information enjoyed by those who are interested in areas such as engineering, land surveying and architecture.
